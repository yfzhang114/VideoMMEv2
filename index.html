Introduction

介绍

In 2024, we released the VideoMME dataset as the first comprehensive benchmark for evaluating video understanding capabilities of multimodal large models. Following its release, VideoMME has driven significant progress in the field of multimodal video understanding. As video understanding models continue to advance, we see them moving from simple video recognition toward deeper semantic understanding and complex reasoning, which places higher demands on evaluation. However, existing methods still focus largely on narrow, task-specific benchmarks, making it difficult to determine whether models truly understand video content or merely exploit dataset biases. Meanwhile, in complex video reasoning tasks, prior work has focused on evaluating specific reasoning abilities while paying less attention to models' overall reasoning coherence, leaving us uncertain whether models perform systematic reasoning or merely generate plausible-looking answers. These limitations hinder comprehensive evaluation of state-of-the-art video understanding models and underscore the need to probe their true understanding and reliable reasoning capabilities. Therefore, we present Video-MME v2. Through over six months of data collection, cleaning, annotation, and curation, we hope to share our in-depth perspectives on next-generation video understanding evaluation at this critical juncture, and to advance further technical progress in the field.

🚀 First Principles

In Video-MME v2, we address two questions:

  • What capabilities should a strong video understanding model possess?
  • How can these capabilities be correctly and adequately evaluated?

To this end, we propose: (1) a complete capability evaluation framework—Video-MME v2 achieves full coverage from single-point information aggregation to temporal understanding to complex video reasoning; (2) a robust evaluation mechanism—group-based evaluation that emphasizes model robustness beyond scatter-shot individual-question assessment.

1. Capability Evaluation Framework

We divide video understanding into three progressive dimensions:

Level 1

Point-wise Information Aggregation

Emphasizes integrating information at one or more fixed timestamps—the foundation of visual perception.

Level 2

Temporal Understanding

Focuses on solving temporally-related tasks and capturing dynamic changes.

Level 3

Complex Reasoning

Introduces complex reasoning in real, diverse scenarios to examine true model capabilities.

This framework integrates existing video evaluation datasets with our reflections on deployable models, comprehensively assessing models from foundational to real-world levels.

2. Group-based Evaluation

We construct two important task groupings:

🎯

Capability Consistency

Evaluates a single capability across different breadths and granularities.

Together these groupings assess robustness and comprehensiveness.

🚀 第一性原理

在Video-MME-v2中，我们从第一性原理出发，回答了两个问题：

  • 一个好的视频理解模型需要什么能力？
  • 这些能力如何得到正确的、充分的评估？

为此，我们提出了：(1) 完整的能力评估体系，通过构建 Multi-level 的视频理解能力评估体系，Video-MME-v2 实现了多点信息聚合—时序信息理解—时序复杂推理的全方位评价体系；(2) 鲁棒的评估机制，引入基于任务分组的评测方式，以问题组为单位的评估更进一步强调模型能力的鲁棒性。

1. 能力评估体系

我们将视频理解划分为三个渐进的维度：

Level 1

多点信息聚合

考察模型对多个离散时间点内视觉特征的提取与整合能力。要求模型能够精准定位并识别特定帧内的物体、属性及场景要素，这是视频理解的基础感知层。

Level 2

时序信息理解

聚焦于视频的动态演变与因果关联。模型需具备长时序上下文建模能力，能够正确解析动作序列、状态变化以及事件发生的先后逻辑等时间强相关信息。

Level 3

时序复杂推理

构建真实视频场景中的高阶认知任务。模型不仅能处理多模态信息，还需结合世界知识、社会习惯等高阶能力进行多跳推理，以应对真实场景中非结构化、高复杂度的理解挑战。

这一评价体系融合了已有的视频评估数据集分类以及我们对于在真实场景中可用的视频理解模型的思考，从而全方位地检验模型从基础到落地的真实水平。

2. 基于任务分组的评测方式

我们构建了两种重要的任务分组：

🎯

能力一致性

旨在验证模型在特定能力域下的鲁棒性。通过构建多粒度与多跨度的测试样本，在不同上下文长度及视觉复杂度下，对同一核心能力（如视频计数、动作理解）进行压力测试。这能有效区分模型是真正掌握了该能力，还是仅在特定分布下表现良好。

🔗

推理连贯性

侧重于评估模型思维链的严密性。不仅关注最终答案的准确性，更引入了中间过程监督机制，对推理路径中的关键逻辑节点进行显式验证。旨在确保模型的输出是基于正确的因果推导，而非源于语言模型的统计相关性猜测。

这两种分组策略相辅相成，共同构成了对模型鲁棒性与全链路推理能力的全面评估，使得视频理解的评估从结果正确迈向过程正确。

3. 视频标注与模型评估

我们精心设计了基于人工检验的数据标注流程以及基于问题分组的模型评估流程：

🎯

视频标注

构建了全链路严谨的基于人工标注的流水线。严格筛选高时效性数据以规避数据污染；采用 Group-based 策略构建包含显式推理链的问答对，并引入 8 选项对抗性设计以降低随机猜测基线；最终通过 Text-Only 测试与多轮交叉校验消除语言先验与主观偏差，确保标注数据的多模态强依赖性与逻辑完备性。

🔗

模型评估

建立以问题组为核心单元的 Group-based Evaluation 体系。引入渐进式一致性加权，通过非线性分数增益衡量模型在同一问题组内的鲁棒性。针对逻辑链，实施首错截断的评分机制，以首个逻辑错误作为置信度边界，杜绝错误前提下的伪正确，确保评估指标具备高度的因果保真度。

严谨的人工介入标注流水线与对抗性层级评估体系构成了双重质检闭环。二者协同作用，将评估维度从单一的结果准确率提升至全链路理解推理鲁棒性，为多模态模型的真实认知能力确立了高置信度的度量基准。

To comprehensively evaluate the video understanding capabilities of multimodal large models, Video-MME V2 has constructed a progressive hierarchical classification system. We have abandoned flat task stacking and instead divided capability dimensions into three cognitive stages: from basic information retrieval and aggregation, advancing to dynamic capture of temporal sequences, actions, and changes, and ultimately rising to complex reasoning about plots, the physical world, and social behaviors.
